    Staff Recommended Beers

    Like at any bar, sometimes it's best to ask what the best beer on tap is. We might not be bartenders, but we do have a few favorite local brews that we think you’ll enjoy. Check out the top 5 best local beers from our own personal recommendations!

    Stone Brewing's Ruination IPA

    Stone Brewing's Ruination IPA is one of Stone Brewery’s finest IPAs. Its extra hoppy bitterness is famous for surprising any of its victims with a swift kick to the palate. Check out all the stats on the main page to learn more about the Ruination!

    Visit external site for more information

    Karl Strauss’s Double Barrel IPA

    One of Karl Strauss’s prized IPAs, the Double Barrel IPA is a great beer for any newbie or expert when it comes to beer. Its rich tropical aroma complements its extra hoppy pop. The page from Karl Strauss features the Double Barrel IPA’s availability in addition to some taste notes and ingredients.

    Visit external site for more information

    Alesmith’s Speedway Stout

    If the Speedway’s multiple awards don't impress you, its initial kick of coffee and chocolate complete with full malty, caramel finish will. Check out the Speedway’s page to see specific beer information and what stores sell Speedway Stout near you.

    Visit external site for more information

    Green Flash’s Le Freak

    Green Flash’s Le Freak is one of the most popular and award winning ales in California. The Le Freak is a blend like no other, comprised of the Belgian Tripel and American Imperial IPA. This page from Green Flash’s beer list shows a brief description of Le Freak’s basic facts, its festival awards, and an option to find where to buy it.

    Visit external site for more information

    Green Flash's Palate Wrecker

    Green Flash’s Palate Wrecker is one of the first west coast IPAs that the Green Flash brewery ever brewed but lives off its reputation for packing a hop-filled punch to the palate. See more detailed beer statistics, awards, and information on where to buy the Palate Wrecker on Green Flash's site.

    Visit external site for more information
