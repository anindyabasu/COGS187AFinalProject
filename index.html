    Welcome

    This site was designed with craft beer beginners in mind. If you’re new to the San Diego craft beer scene, have no fear because SD beer is here!

    We know how hard it is to jump straight into craft beer, so we have compiled all the best information for those beer newbies looking to expand their beer proficiency.

    On this site, we provide all the beer information you’ll need to get started on learning about craft beer. You’ll find information ranging from some of the newest and most popular craft brews of San Diego, the most popular breweries, pubs, and events in the area, to some useful basics that will have you identifying beer and talking intelligently about any beer in no time.

    Peruse our site and take your first steps towards becoming a true San Diego craft beer connoisseur.

    Enjoy!
