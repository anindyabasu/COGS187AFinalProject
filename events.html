    Events & Tours

    There are tons of beer-related events in San Diego, including brewery tours or occasions for beer enthusiasts to gather and enjoy some craft beers! Find a tour to visit a brewery to learn more about the brewing process or check out the festivities!

    Brewery Tours

    Brewery Tours of San Diego offers tours for everyday of the week for those interested in seeing multiple breweries in a given time frame. Their tours range in price from $75-$95 based on the day of the week and the number of breweries visited. Every tour provides transportation, bottled waters, and a souvenir beer glass to boot.

    Visit external site for more information

    St. Patrick’s Day @ Monkey Paw

    St. Patrick’s Day is famous not just for wearing green but also for drinking with friends and family. Monkey Paw Pub & Brewery is hosting their own St. Patrick’s Day event on Monday, March 17th and is serving some of their very own Irish inspired Stouts, food specials, and plenty of drafts.

    Visit external site for more information

    Taps & Tapas

    Looking for a casual place to drink a few brews, enjoy some great food, and chat up some locals? The Bier Garden’s Taps & Tapas in Encinitas offers a wide variety of craft beers and great food pairings with excellent local tapas and occurs weekly from 5AM to 10PM.

    Visit external site for more information

    First Annual Bikes & Beers SD

    If you’re an avid biker, check out Bikes & Beers SD on March 29th! The first annual Bikes & Beers aims to bring attention to the San Diego cycling scene with a morning of biking and followed by some well-deserved brews. The first group of bikers leave from North Park at 8AM. The site offers bike routes and more related information.

    Visit external site for more information

    Upcoming Events

    Taphunter’s list of local events in San Diego’s consists of a comprehensive listing of events ranging from small and casual to famous annual events. Check out the their full events listing and get started on meeting new people while drinking some great local beers.
