    Ballast Point

    Priding itself on being one of the biggest San Diego breweries, Ballast point is recognized for their gold medal winning IPAs. The site includes a list of their popular award winning beers, along with the availability, beers facts, and brewer’s notes for each beer. You can also see facts on how to visit any of its 3 locations to sample or buy their beers.

    Recommended

    Sculpin IPA

    Big Eye IPA

    Dorado IPA

    Calico Amber Ale

    Visit external site for more information

    Green Flash

    An up and coming San Diego brewery located in Mira Mesa, Green Flash Brewing and its staff pride itself on its numerous award winning Ales and Pale Ales. If these awards shed a (green) light on their quality beers, you can schedule a tour for $5 dollar public tours to sample their latest beers or find out what stores near you sell Gren Flash brews.

    Recommended

    Imperial IPA

    Palate Wrecker

    West Coast IPA

    Le Freak

    Visit external site for more information

    Karl Strauss

    a leader in San Diego brewing of IPAs, Ambers, and Ales, Karl Straus and its 8 breweries produce nationally acclaimed craft beer. The site offers a detailed description of the statistics, ingredients, and awards for all of their beers. If this sounds interesting, you can also find the location and hours of their tasting rooms to come out and try any of their 20 on tap beers.

    Recommended

    Big Barrel Double IPA

    Wreck Alley Imperial Stout

    Red Trolley Ale

    Off the Rails

    Visit external site for more information

    Stone Brew

    One of San Diego’s most popular and acclaimed breweries, Stone Brewing is famous for their IPAs. The site offers a full listing of its various types of released beers, their statistics, and links to reviews. In addition to showing upcoming events, the site gives directions to schedule a $3 daily tours, or to make a reservation at either of their world class bistros.

    Recommended

    Smoked Porter

    Ruination IPA

    Stone IPA

    Arrogant Bastard Ale

    Visit external site for more information
