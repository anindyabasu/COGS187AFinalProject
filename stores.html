    Olive Tree Market

    Beer Market

    With only one location, Olive Tree Market might not be the option you’re looking for if convenience is an issue. However, Olive Tree Market makes up for this by providing customers with not only a wide selection of craft beers but also an extensive inventory of foods that go well with beer, like its artisanal cheeses.

    Visit external site for more information

    Whole Foods Market

    Local Retailer with Craft Beers

    Though limited in its selection of popular domestic beers, Whole Foods has an extensive and diverse selection of local and international craft beers. This page shows you some of Whole Foods’ featured beers and a couple of Beer 101 videos on pouring and brewing beer. Whole Foods has four major locations within San Diego, including La Jolla, Del Mar, Encinitas, and Hillcrest.

    Visit external site for more information

    Half Time Beverage

    Online Retailer

    Half Time Beverage is a great online resource for finding and buying beer from one of the world’s largest selection of international and craft beers. In addition, the site offers great filtering options, like filtering by beer color, cost, country, brewery, and more.

    Visit external site for more information

    RateBeer

    Store Listing

    RateBeer is one of the best when it comes to providing lists of the best breweries, bars, and stores for craft beers. The site provides ratings, reviews, location, and even the number of different brands by bottle sold at each store. If you’re hoping to find the top rated and reviewed craft beer stores in San Diego, then this might the be the place for you.

    Visit external site for more information
