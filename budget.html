    Beer on a Budget

    Want to try different kinds of beer but working with a limited budget? The following sites provide a list of affordable beers for the everyday economic drinker. Browse through a pre-compiled list or customize your own personal search!

    CBS Local

    Affordable Craft Beers

    Though craft beers can be considered pricey, CBS Local shows that drinking quality beer on a budget isn’t as hard as you think. For those craft beer seekers out there, this site provides the most popular inexpensive, as well as widely available, craft beers and a brief description of each.

    Visit external site for more information

    Liquor Mart

    Easily Searchable Beers

    Liquor Mart is an online liquor service that enables easy searching using its various, useful filter options through its vast beer database. If price is a concern, this site is a great resource as it easily illustrates beer prices by brand through filtering and shows the latest beer sales via its side navigation.
