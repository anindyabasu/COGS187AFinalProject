    Safety

    While beer is a great way to relax, it's always important to stay safe! The following sites cover topics ranging from alcohol safety to alcohol poisoning. Take the necessary precautions to ensure that you don't endanger yourself or others, and know the signs of alcohol poisoning!

    Tips for Alcohol Safety

    CSULB offers sensible tips on drinking safely! It is important to keep yourself and those around you safe while consuming alcoholic beverages. Follow this guide for tips on staying safe while being able to enjoy a beer at the same time!

    Mayo Clinic

    Mayo Clinic is a great resource for health-related issues, alcohol is no exception. Here, Mayo Clinic can help anyone recognize the symptoms, causes, risks, and even prevention measures for alcohol poisoning.

    Medical News Today

    Medical News Today offers a more straightforward approach to explaining alcohol poisoning. By placing attention on the major symptoms and risks and a few of important treatment measures, this site aims at educate anyone partaking in drinking.
