    Popular Local Beers

    If you’re diving into the San Diego beer scene, you might want a few pointers on which beers to sip on. This list of San Diego’s Top 10 most popular craft beers takes the best brews from San Diego’s finest breweries, each beer’s reviews, and some similar local and national craft beers, and puts them in one place for your viewing pleasure.

    1. Alesmith Speedway Stout

    At the top of our list is the Speedway Stout, a stout that’s combination of creamy coffee and roasted marshmallow flavors seems without equal. This stout from Alesmith Brewery has certainly made a name for itself as one of the best. Check out more on Alesmith’s page to see more in-depth beer information and what stores sell Speedway Stout near you, or check out some (reviews) on ratebeer.

    The Lost Abbey’s Angel Share unique blend of vanilla, oak, rum cake and raisins has turned the heads of many San Diego craft beer enthusiasts. See more about this brew’s unique ingredients, tasting notes, and history on Lost Abbey’s page. Still not sold? Check out some of the Angel Share’s (reviews) on ratebeer.

    3. Green Flash Double Stout

    Looking for a luscious black brew’s with a smooth finish? Enter the Green Flash Double Stout. This double stout has become famous worldwide for its bold flavor, winning silver in the world beer cup. The Green Flash’s site shows more about the Double Stout’s beer statistics, awards & accolades, and how to buy it. Feel free to see some reviews at ratebeer.com.
