    How-to

    For some, beer is more than just an end-of-the-day, kick-off-your-shoes drink, but for many San Diegans, beer is an art form that requires proper education. Educate yourself with these fun how-to’s that will help you learn everything from pouring beer to pairing it with food, and you'll soon be passing as an expert in no time.

    How to Enjoy the Varying Beer Taste

    A great overview and guide to what steps are necessary to enjoy a beer, the article from About covers just about everything you could want to find in a beer. For beginners, the article starts off basic, teaching some fundamentals of beer types and their characteristics and national origins, and then gets as specific as recommending glasses and temperatures for serving beer.

    Visit external site for more information

    How to Properly Taste Beer

    Beer is meant to be enjoyed, and Draft Magazine discusses steps for doing just that. The site’s steps range from picking the proper glass temperature to finally tasting the beer and tips on how to discuss it. See more about enjoying your beer!

    Visit external site for more information

    How to Pour like Pro

    Pouring beer is an art form in and of itself. WikiHow’s easy to read, step-by-step pictures and explanations help identify what position to pour beer, what angle to aim for, how quickly to pour, and knowing the right time to stop. Whether you’re a novice or an enthusiast looking to refine your skill, knowing how to pour a beer is essential.

    Visit external site for more information

    How to Pair your Food and Beer

    Some foods compliment specific styles of beer well; others contrast these styles. Here, Epicurious explains how foods can bring out or clash with beer tastes and also gives examples of of common foods that match up well with particular styles of beer.
