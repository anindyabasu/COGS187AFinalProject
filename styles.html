    IPA (Indian Pale Ale)

    IPA is perhaps the most popular style of beer in the San Diego area, with local craft brews like Stone Brewing and Ballast Point available on tap or bottled at almost any SD bar. This page gives a great 101 on everything IPA, covering flavors, important ingredients like hops, sub-types of IPAs, and more.

    Brands to Try

    Goose Island's Indian Pale Ale

    Samuel Smith's Indian Pale Ale

    Stone Brewing's IPA
