    Newest & Trending

    Interested in trying the newest beers available? The following sites provide a list of newest beers and where to acquire them! You can find beers ranging from local beers to international beers at a variety of different places and keep up to date on the newest trending beers!

    San Diego Magazine

    Latest on Local Beers

    San Diego Magazine shows a list of the most popular and newest releases of beer from the some of the top breweries in the local San Diego area. In addition to brief discussions of the newest beers, you can also find helpful notes on where to sample or purchase these new releases.

    Visit external site for more information

    Craft Beer

    Seasonal Beers

    Here, Craft Beer can help fill you in on the latest seasonal beers. Additionally, Craft Beer includes details about each beer, such as the alcohol content, some of the ingredients and hops used, and the brewery that produced the beer. The site also lists where each beer is distributed for purchase.

    Visit external site for more information

    Half Time Beverage

    Newest International Beers

    Hoping to find the newest international craft beers? Offering one of the largest selections of craft beer from all over the world, Halftime Beverages makes searching for the newest beer releases a walk in the park by providing filters based on date and including options to view price and in-depth beer details.

    Visit external site for more information

    Beer Advocate

    Newest Beers

    If you’re a beer enthusiast looking for the latest and greatest in beer releases, Beer Advocate's listing of the newest beer releases may be perfect for you. Ranging from new beer releases to new brand packaging to new bottle sizes, Beer Advocate provides the latest on any new beer-related releases.

    Visit external site for more information
