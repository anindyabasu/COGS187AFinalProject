    Newest/Trending

    Find out what's hot!

    San Diego Magazine | Latest on Local Beers

    San Diego Magazine shows a list of the most popular newest releases of beer from the some of the top breweries in the local San Diego area. In addition to the brief discussion of the new beer, you can find helpful notes on where to sample or purchase these releases.

    Visit external site for more information

    Craft Beer | Seasonal Beer

    Here, Craft Beer can help fill you in on the latest seasonal beers. Additionally, craft beers gives details on the alcohol content, a few of the ingredients and hops used for each beer, as well as which brewery produced each beer and where the each is distributed for purchase.

    Visit external site for more information

    Halftime Beverage |

    Hoping to find the newest international craft beers? Offering one of the largest selections of craft beer from all over the world, Half Time Beverages makes search for newest beer releases a walk in the park by providing filters based on date by days and options to view price and in-depth beer details.

    Visit external site for more information

    Beer Advocate |

    If you’re a beer enthusiast looking for all the latest and greatest on beer releases, beeradvocate’s listing newest beer releases may be perfect for you. Ranging from new beer releases to new brand packaging to new bottle sizes, beeradvocate shows almost any new beer-related releases.

    Visit external site for more information
