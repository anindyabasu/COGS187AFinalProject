    Bars

    (each of these bars offers an extensive craft beer menu)

    Hamilton's Tavern

    Considered to be the first archetypal beer bar of San Diego, Hamilton's Tavern in South Park has 28 beers on tap and a combination of local, national, and international bottled beers. The decor is that of a traditional neighborhood alehouse, giving it a historical feel. Check out the site for directions and their full menu of beers.

    "Great atmosphere, awesome decor and the best selection of draught beers."

    - Yelp Reviewer

    See more reviews on Yelp

    Visit external site for more information

    Regal Beagle

    Admired for its great beer selection and excellent sweet potato fries, the Regal Beagle located in Mission Hills is a bar that doesn’t hurt the wallet. Want to see what others have said? See reviews, hours, menus, and more on Yelp.

    "Plenty of seating and tv's. Had an IPA, lamb sausage, tots, and the beagle balls. The atmosphere is great, chill vibe. The staff is awesome, the food is great."

    - Yelp Reviewer

    See more reviews on Yelp

    Visit external site for more information

    O'Brien's Pub

    With over 20 different brands on tap and over 100 different bottled brands, O’Brien’s is an ideal stop to sip a beer if you’re ever passing through Convoy. If O’Brien’s massive beer list doesn’t catch your eye, maybe their food and numerous Irish soccer fans will! Check out their Yelp reviews or go straight to their website.

    "Wonderful selection, always have Pliny the Elder on tap, show all soccer matches, Delicious food. Obrien's is MY bar/pub of choice when in San Diego."

    - Yelp Reviewer

    See more reviews on Yelp

    Visit external site for more information

    The Loft

    The Loft is a great music venue and bar for any UCSD student or nearby resident looking to enjoy unique music and great beer. Zanzibar at the Loft, located on campus at UCSD, offers an eclectic variety of bottled beers and handcrafted microbrews, including famous local, nationwide, and international craft beers.

    "My new favorite place to eat at on campus from 4-6pm. As for the food, everything is so flavorful and filling. And of course, their happy hour and beer selection are always things to look forward to."

    - Yelp Reviewer

    See more reviews on Yelp
